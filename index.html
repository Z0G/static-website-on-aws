Meet The Author

Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Digital Wellness Expert & Behavioral Psychologist

Dr. Sarah Mitchell is a leading voice in digital wellness and behavioral psychology. With over 15 years of research experience at Stanford University, she has helped thousands of individuals and organizations create healthier relationships with technology.

Her work has been featured in The New York Times, TED, and Harvard Business Review. She holds a Ph.D. in Psychology and is the founder of the Digital Mindfulness Institute.
